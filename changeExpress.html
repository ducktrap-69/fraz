The Transformation You Require

Professionals who can look beyond their own profession are often capable to manage different processes of change at the same time. They have become aware of the fact that performance in the global business world requires more than just doing your job. It requires more than a professional mindset, and more skills and professional competences. This awareness triggers two questions: what do I need more than just my own experience, or my current professionalism? How to develop these experiences in a shorter period of time? It is for sure that the managers and professionals, responsible for change and innovation besides their own expertise, will have to find an answer.

The training and coaching program The Change Express will stimulate the process of looking beyond one’s own profession and discover what needs to be learned more. A practical set of training modules including tools, will support the (desired) learnings and required competences to manage challenging changes in organizations, especially when they occur unexpectedly, and immediate action is required. The Change Express training and coaching program provides people and their organizations with the boost required to realize their way forward. The acquired skills will remain for life.

Training and coaching program The Change Express
This program consists of three main modules, Know Thyself (individual level), Mind the Gap (team level), and Embrace the Future (organizational and societal level).

KNOW
THYSELF
The training Know Thyself will support you in exploring (hidden) competences. You will reflect upon yourself, develop your ability to anticipate business relationships, and increase your level of empathy and imagination
MIND
THE GAP
The training Mind The Gap consists of a management game/ simulation which increases people's skills on how to manage future scenarios and ambitions.
EMBRACE
THE FUTURE
The training Embrace The Future will focus on learning and applying tools to manage change. People will structure challenges in such a way that the change will require minimum of effort for maximum return.

OUR APPROACHPATH TO SUCCESS

Exploration & Self-Understanding

Embark on a journey of self-discovery through informal discussions and trust-building. Understand challenges and set goals to bridge the gap between your current and desired professional state.
STEP 1

Development & Transformation

Dive deeper into self-awareness. Share experiences, learn from practical examples, and acquire essential tools. Engage in creative activities, simulations, and develop leadership skills to bring your vision to life.

STEP 2

Strategy & Implementation

Translate your insights into actionable steps. Test and refine your plans, measuring their impact on your business. Implement changes and innovations while continually monitoring progress.

STEP 3

Sustainable Growth

Evaluate the results of your efforts, making necessary adjustments. Standardize successful processes and celebrate achievements to promote results in the market.

STEP 4

Continuous Improvement & Leadership

Foster a culture of ongoing development. Reflect on progress, identify new focus points, and sustain the momentum of change, all while providing leadership and guidance.
