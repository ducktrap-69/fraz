FEMALE LEADERSHIP

Storytelling

Since 2017 we are conducting a variety of dialogues and storytelling sessions with female leaders who are managing complex change processes in their organizations. Female leaders with different backgrounds and nationalities, with different passions for their expertise, different experiences and with different views about what organisations and life is all about. Stories from the perspective of a fast-changing world. The purpose of this applied research is to explore hidden talents and competences of female leaders, while driving change.

The dialogue

During these dialogues we touch upon different topics, and matters. What can we learn from these female leaders? What kind of changes are they exactly dealing with? What was the impact of these changes on their personal and professional life? How did they lead the process during tough and unpredictable moments? Why is their drive to make the change succeed? What were their key-learnings after finalizing the change process? And, did they have any doubts or on the contrary felt motivated for another new change? We ask these questions to female leaders in order for them to reflect on leadership moments and share their leadership style, their competences and their choice whether to lead more changes or not.
